Privacy Policy for JoJo Cam

Last Updated: May 25, 2026

Your privacy is highly important. This Privacy Policy explains how JoJo Cam ("the App") handles information when you use the mobile application.

1. Information Collected

JoJo Cam is built with a commitment to data minimization. We do not require any account registration, nor do we collect any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location).

To help maintain app stability and improve performance, the App may collect limited, non-identifiable technical data:

  • Diagnostics: Crash data, performance metrics (such as app launch time or energy usage), and other diagnostic data.
  • Usage Data: Product interaction data (such as anonymized logs of features used within the App) and other general usage data.

2. How Data is Used

Any technical data collected is used solely for Analytics and Performance Optimization purposes. This data helps debug technical issues, prevent crashes, and improve the overall user experience of JoJo Cam.

3. Data Linkage and Tracking

No Linkage: All diagnostic and usage data collected is completely stripped of direct identifiers before it is processed. This data is not linked to your identity or your device.

No Tracking: We do not track you across third-party websites or applications, nor is your data used for targeted advertising.

4. Security of Data

While no personal data is stored, any technical information transmitted to automated crash-reporting systems is protected using industry-standard security and encryption protocols.

5. Children's Privacy

Because JoJo Cam does not collect any personal information, the App complies completely with children's privacy regulations, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly target or collect information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted directly on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or the security of the App, please contact us via the support page.
