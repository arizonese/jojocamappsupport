About JoJo Cam

Learn more about our mission and technology

Our Mission

JoJo Cam was created with a single goal in mind: to provide a simple, secure, and private way for people to connect their devices directly. In an age where privacy is increasingly important, we believe that camera communication should never require surrendering your personal data or trusting third-party servers.

How It Works

JoJo Cam uses peer-to-peer (P2P) technology to establish direct connections between devices. This means:

  • No Server Required: Devices communicate directly with each other
  • No Data Collection: We don't store or have access to your camera feeds
  • End-to-End Encryption: All communications are encrypted between devices
  • Privacy by Design: No account registration needed, no tracking, no ads

Technology Stack

JoJo Cam is built with modern technologies:

  • Flutter: Cross-platform mobile development for iOS and Android
  • WebRTC: Industry-standard P2P communication protocol
  • End-to-End Encryption: Secure data transmission

Privacy Commitment

Your privacy is our top priority. JoJo Cam:

  • Collects zero personal information
  • Does not require account creation
  • Never stores or accesses your camera feeds
  • Does not track you across apps or websites
  • Is fully compliant with privacy regulations including COPPA

Support & Feedback

We're constantly improving JoJo Cam based on user feedback. If you have any questions, encounter any issues, or have suggestions for improvement, please don't hesitate to contact us.
